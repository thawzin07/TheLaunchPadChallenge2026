# Credits, Tools, And Sponsors

## AI Quick Summary

Registered participants receive sponsor credits and API access. OpenAI and GMI Cloud credits require Luma registration plus a Google Form sent after registration. Agnes AI credits are available by signing up directly on the Agnes AI platform. Prize credits are separate from participant credits.

## Credit Claiming

| Provider | How to claim | Publicly visible? | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| OpenAI | Register through Luma, then fill the Google Form sent after registration | Form is not public | Use the same email throughout |
| GMI Cloud | Register through Luma, then fill the Google Form sent after registration | Form is not public | Use the same email throughout |
| Agnes AI | Create an account at `https://platform.agnes-ai.com` | Public sign-up path is visible | Credits are attached on sign-up |

If credits do not arrive, contact the organizing team at `acaciaaisociety@gmail.com`.

## Sponsor Descriptions

### Agnes AI

Agnes AI is described on the challenge page as a Singapore-based AI model company that designs, trains, and deploys full-modality foundation models in-house. The page states that Agnes AI is built and trained in Singapore and listed on the Artificial Analysis global benchmark leaderboard.

### OpenAI

OpenAI is listed as the company behind GPT and Codex and as a sponsor supporting participants with API credits.

### GMI Cloud

GMI Cloud is listed as an NVIDIA Platform Cloud Partner providing GPU infrastructure for production AI, including serverless inference and dedicated clusters.

## Important Distinction

Participant build credits and prize credits are different:

- Participant credits: help teams build during the challenge; exact issued amounts may depend on registration/form responses.
- Prize credits: awarded to winning teams, with amounts listed in the prize table.

## Access Caveats

- The OpenAI/GMI Google Form is sent after Luma registration and is not publicly visible from the challenge page.
- Luma registration is subject to approval.
- Luma exact event address is hidden until registration.
- Participants should check their registered email inbox and spam folder for credit instructions.
